Choose Your Boba

There are two players. The player whose turn it is chooses two cups of boba. The total number of boba balls is added to the current player's score, unless either cup comes up with only one boba ball. If this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is now the other player’s turn. After each turn, the current player can either choose again, (assuming they did not choose a cup with only one boba ball) or if the current player feels that luck is running thin, they can pass to the other player. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

Oh, and if you pick two cups with only one boba ball AKA Snake Eyes, your current score gets zeroed out. So don’t do that.
